FULL DESCRIPTION  (Google Play long description)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
ColorPath Out is a bright, colorful arrow path puzzle that challenges your logic, planning, and spatial thinking — without timers or pressure.

Your goal is simple: tap each colorful arrow so it slides off the board. An arrow escapes only when the path from its tip to the edge is clear. Tap the wrong one and you lose a heart. Clear every arrow to win the level.

If you love calming brain games, offline logic puzzles, and satisfying “whoosh” moments when a blocked board suddenly opens up, ColorPath Out is built for you.

HOW TO PLAY
• Study the colorful paths on the grid
• Find an arrow with a free exit to the edge
• Tap to slide it out and open space for the others
• Plan the order carefully — blocked taps cost hearts
• Use a hint when you’re stuck
• Clear every arrow to complete the level

WHY COLORPATH OUT FEELS DIFFERENT
Most arrow escape games lean black-and-white and minimal. ColorPath Out leans colorful. Every level is a vivid path puzzle you can read at a glance, with multiple color themes so you can play sharp and focused or soft and relaxed.

FEATURES
• Hundreds of handcrafted colorful arrow path levels
• Smooth tap-to-escape controls and clear wrong-move feedback
• Hearts, undo, restart, and smart hints
• Multiple visual themes
• No timers — think at your own pace
• Offline play — no Wi‑Fi required after install
• Quick sessions for a break, or longer sessions for deep focus

PERFECT FOR
• Fans of arrow puzzle, path puzzle, and logic escape games
• Players who want a relaxing offline brain game
• Anyone who prefers colorful visuals over stark monochrome grids
• Short coffee-break challenges and longer puzzle evenings

Train your brain, protect your hearts, and clear every colorful path.

Download ColorPath Out and see how far you can go with a perfect heart score.

6) 90-DAY PLAN TOWARD 100,000 DOWNLOADS
================================================================================

PHASE 0 — PRE-LAUNCH (Week −2 to 0)
  □ Change applicationId to com.appwaretech.colorpathout
  □ Privacy Policy + Terms URLs live
  □ Data safety form accurate (Ads + Device IDs if using AdMob)
  □ 80–150+ handcrafted levels minimum at launch (more = retention)
  □ Crash-free sessions target ≥ 99.5%
  □ Closed testing → 12+ testers → 14 days if required by new Play account
  □ Soft-launch countries ready (see Phase 1)

PHASE 1 — SOFT LAUNCH (Days 1–30) | Countries: Philippines, Indonesia, Pakistan, Egypt, Turkey
  Goals (exit criteria before global push):
    D1 retention ≥ 35–40%
    D7 retention ≥ 15–18%
    Avg rating ≥ 4.5
    Play Store conversion (store page → install) ≥ 25–30% after creative polish

  Actions:
    • $15–40/day Google UAC or App campaigns (learning + retention data)
    • Ask soft-launch players for reviews after a win (in-app review API — already in project)
    • Fix ad frequency if D1 dies (common puzzle killer)
    • Ship weekly level packs

PHASE 2 — CREATIVE ENGINE (Days 15–60) | TikTok / Reels / Shorts
  Post 1–2 short clips daily:
    Hook: wrong taps → hearts → perfect clear in 8–12 seconds
    Text overlays: “Colorful arrow path puzzle” / “No timer”
  Whitelist best organic clips into paid UA
  Target interests: puzzle, brain games, ASMR satisfying clears

PHASE 3 — GLOBAL + SCALE (Days 31–90)
  Expand to US / UK / CA / AU / DE / BR once soft-launch KPI gates pass
  Budget guide for ~100K installs in 90 days (varies wildly by CPI):
    Low CPI regions: $0.10–0.40
    Tier-1: $0.80–2.50+
  Rough math: blending soft + global, a $3,000–$8,000 total UA spend
  + strong organic ASO often covers the 100K goal; pure organic is possible
  but not reliable.

CONTENT / PRODUCT UPGRADES THAT MOVE RANKINGS
  Week 2: Daily challenge mode
  Week 4: Level pack + “shape levels” (stars, animals, icons — Arrow Wave style)
  Week 6: Achievements / streak
  Week 8: More themes + accessibility (colorblind palette)
  Ongoing: Fair ads (rewarded hint, interstitial only between levels — never mid-solve)

MONETIZATION THAT HELPS (not hurts) downloads
  ✓ Rewarded video for hint / +1 heart
  ✓ Optional remove-ads IAP (price competitively vs Easybrain’s $15 complaints)
  ✗ Forced double ads after every level
  ✗ Fake cash reward / withdrawal claims
