2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

ColorPath Out is primarily an offline puzzle game. We do not require you to
create an account, and we do not ask for your name, email address, phone number,
or other personal registration details to play.

2.1 Information stored on your device (locally)
    The App may store the following on your device using local storage
    (for example SharedPreferences):

    • Game progress (levels completed, scores, lives/hearts, hints)
    • Settings preferences (sound, vibration, theme, and similar options)
    • App review / update related flags

    This data stays on your device. We do not upload it to our own servers.

2.2 Information collected by advertising partners
    The App displays ads through Google AdMob (Google LLC). Ad partners may
    automatically collect certain information from your device, which may
    include:

    • Advertising ID / device identifiers
    • IP address
    • Device type, operating system, and app version
    • Approximate location derived from IP address
    • Ad interaction data (views, clicks)

    This information is collected and processed by Google under their own
    privacy policies. We do not control how Google uses this data.

    Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
    How Google uses data from apps: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

2.3 Information we do NOT collect ourselves
    We do not operate our own analytics backend for this App. We do not sell
    your personal information. We do not collect precise GPS location, contacts,
    photos, microphone audio, or camera data for this game.
